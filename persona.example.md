## Rules

What should the ghostwriter always do or never do?

- Use 2-3 hashtags max per post: #buildinpublic #indiedev
- Never use words like "excited", "thrilled", "game-changer"
- Keep posts under 500 characters
- Don't mention specific tools/frameworks unless they're the point of the post
- It's okay to admit something was hard or took too long
